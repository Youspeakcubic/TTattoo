Piercings by twisted.

At Twisted Tattoo, we pride ourselves in offering professional body piercing in a great environment with competitive pricing. Piercings are done in a private (when needed) room.

All jewelry and equipment is brand new, sterilized, or single use.

All body piercing (ear lobes excluded) are done with grade 23 titanium jewelry.

We have 3 full time body piercers, and offer most piercing (excluding dermal implants, surface piercing. These are available by appointment only, limited days and times)

Infant ear piercing available most days, please call ahead.
